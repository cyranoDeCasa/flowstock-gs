Privacy Policy

1. Data Collected

The FlowStock application is a desktop application running offline. No personal data is collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers.

2. Local Use of Information

All information you enter (products, sales, statistics, etc.) is stored only on your device in local files. You retain full control over it.

3. No Tracking or Analytics

FlowStock contains no tracking modules, cookies, or navigation analytics. No internet connection is required or made.

4. Security and Confidentiality

Users are encouraged to regularly back up their local data. FlowStock does not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding privacy, you can contact us at: flowstockgs@gmail.com
