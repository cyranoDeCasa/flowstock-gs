5 Common Inventory Management Mistakes

Poor inventory management can be costly for your business. Here are the most common mistakes we see among retailers and SMEs — and how to avoid them.

1. Not Tracking Stock Movements in Real Time

Many businesses still rely on weekly or monthly reports. The result: unexpected stockouts. With FlowStock, movements are recorded instantly.

2. Lack of Visibility on Dormant Products

Some products remain in stock for months without being sold. It's crucial to monitor these items and adjust your orders accordingly.

3. Poor Communication Between Departments

When inventory management isn't connected to sales or purchasing, errors accumulate. FlowStock facilitates collaboration through a shared dashboard.

4. Incorrect Reorder Thresholds

Not setting minimum stock levels leads to either stockouts or overstocking. Configure automatic alerts to prevent this.

5. Ignoring Reports and Analytics

Data is valuable! Failing to analyze sales and stock movements prevents optimization.
